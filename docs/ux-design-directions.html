Design Philosophy

Authentic Anime Pok√©dex Experience

Every pixel answers the question: "Would Ash have seen this on Dexter's screen?" The holographic blue aesthetic, geometric borders, glowing effects, and retro-futuristic typography recreate the iconic anime Pok√©dex display.

Visual-First Design

Pok√©mon sprites dominate (50-60% of screen space). This isn't a data table with small icons - it's a window into the Pok√©mon world. Information supports the visual, not the other way around.

Appliance Simplicity

No settings menus. No configuration. No help screens. Pick it up and it just works. Button prompts at the bottom show available actions. Navigation is intuitive through exploration.

3-Press Navigation Rule

Any Pok√©mon is reachable within 3 button presses from any screen. Efficient navigation without complexity. L/R switches generations. Up/Down scrolls. A dives deeper. B goes back. That's it.
